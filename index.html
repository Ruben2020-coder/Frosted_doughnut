Glazed Doughnut holes

Requirements-you will need 2 cups confectioners' sugar, 3 to 5 tablespoons frozen grape, cherry-pomegranate or cranberry juice, thawed, 12 doughnut holes, LEMON VARIATION:, 2 cups confectioners' sugar, 5 tablespoons lemon juice

Recipe

Whisk together sugar and enough juice concentrate to achieve a thick glaze. Dip doughnut holes in glaze; transfer to waxed paper. Variation: For lemon glaze, whisk together sugar and lemon juice until smooth. Dip doughnut holes and transfer to waxed paper. 1 doughnut hole: 225 calories, 4g fat, 49g carbohydrate , 1g protein.
Disclaimer: Never leave a child unattended in the kitchen. Limit the child to tasks that are safe and age-appropriate.
